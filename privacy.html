ScanMind Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the ScanMind mobile application ("ScanMind", "the app", "we", "us") handles information. ScanMind is provided by Zubeid Hendricks ("the Developer"). By using ScanMind you agree to this Policy.

ScanMind is built privacy-first: scanning and the first OCR pass run on your device, and your scans are stored on your device. Sign-in is optional, and we do not maintain a copy of your documents.

1. Information ScanMind processes

  • Scans and extracted content. Images you capture or import, the text recognized from them, document titles, detected codes (QR/barcode), on-device search embeddings, and any structured fields or translations you generate. These are stored locally on your device (Apple SwiftData and an app-group container) and, if you enable it in the future, in your private iCloud. We do not receive or store this content on our own servers.
  • API keys. If you add your own Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, or local Ollama details, they are stored only in the iOS Keychain on your device and are used solely to make the requests you initiate.
  • Sign in with Apple (optional). If you choose to sign in, Apple provides a stable identifier and, on first sign-in, the name and email you approve. These are stored on your device (the identifier in the Keychain) to establish your identity for future cross-device sync. You can choose to hide your email via Apple's private relay, and you can sign out at any time in Settings.
  • Subscription status. Your purchase and entitlement state is managed by Apple and read by the app to unlock features. We do not receive your payment card details.
  • Usage counters. A local count of AI actions used per day is stored on your device to apply plan limits. It is not transmitted to us.

2. When information leaves your device

Your scans stay on your device unless you use a feature that requires a cloud service:

  • Cloud OCR, the Ask assistant, structured data extraction, and translation. When the on-device engine is insufficient or you invoke an AI feature, the relevant page image and/or extracted text is sent to the AI provider in use — for example Google Gemini, Anthropic (Claude), or a local Ollama server you control — solely to perform the action you requested. Each provider processes that data under its own privacy policy.
  • Sharing and export. When you share or export a scan or PDF, the content goes wherever you direct it via the iOS share sheet.

If you only use the on-device features (Apple Vision and/or a local Ollama server), no scan content leaves your device through ScanMind.

3. Permissions

  • Camera — to scan documents and handwriting. Used only while scanning.
  • Photos — only the images you choose to import are accessed.
  • Local network — only to reach an Ollama server you configure.

4. Payments and subscriptions

Subscriptions are sold and processed by Apple through the App Store. Billing, renewals, and refunds are handled by Apple under your Apple Account. We do not collect or store your payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. We do not sell your data; no tracking

We do not sell or rent your information. ScanMind does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs and does not track you across other apps or websites. The only third parties that process your content are the service providers you choose to use (the AI providers above) and Apple (for purchases and, optionally, iCloud).

6. Data retention and deletion

Your scans remain on your device until you delete them in the app or delete the app, which removes the locally stored data. Content sent to an AI provider is retained and deleted according to that provider's policy. You can remove your stored API keys at any time in Settings.

7. Security

Data is stored within the app's sandbox and app group; API keys are stored in the iOS Keychain. Network requests to AI providers use HTTPS. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

ScanMind is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country) and we do not knowingly collect their information.

9. International processing

If you use cloud features, your content may be processed on servers operated by the relevant AI provider in countries other than your own, subject to that provider's terms.

10. Your rights

Because your scans are stored on your device, you control them directly — view, edit, export, and delete them in the app. Depending on your location (e.g., the EEA/UK under the GDPR, or California under the CCPA), you may have additional rights regarding personal data; for any data held by an AI provider, contact that provider. To reach us, use the contact below.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date on this page.
